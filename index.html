About us

Inova & Frugal Innovation

The purpose of our organization INOVA lies within the future of frugal innovation. We want to spread the idea that could help all of humanity in gaining more by doing less. We don't always need to come up with new advanced innovations for the purpose to compete with the top products of this world, so instead we could try to make it a bit less complicated for the purpose of making it accessible to everyone. Not everyone has the same need or access to the new expensive products that's coming out to the market, and they probably wouldn't need them for everything that the products can offer either. We can't waste money and resources to create something new, but rather recreate ideas and reuse what we already have. Everything has some kind of value, which we plan on making useful and accessible for everyone.
